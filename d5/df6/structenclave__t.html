Detailed Description

Information about enclave known to the RTI, including its runtime state, mode of execution, and connectivity with other enclaves. The list of upstream and downstream enclaves does not include those that are connected via a "physical" connection (one denoted with ~>) because those connections do not impose any scheduling constraints.
